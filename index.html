How to Play – Privilege & Betrayal

You are racing around a 30‑space track, trying to crack a hidden 5‑digit code.

  • Goal: Be the first player to correctly guess the 5‑digit code.
  • One roll per turn: Roll once, move, resolve the space, then end your turn.
  • Guess spaces: Let you attempt the main code.
  • Card spaces: Auto‑draw a random card.
  • Connections: Every space has a hidden code (1–4). Guess wrong → move back 2.
  • Private chat: Each player has secret notes only visible on their turn.
